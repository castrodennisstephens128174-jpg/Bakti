7 / 9

Why Stellar; why the provider path

Verifiable rails now; regulated last mile next

Why Stellar for this prototype

  • Freighter keeps signing with the sender
  • Horizon and Soroban RPC make transfers inspectable
  • Soroban demonstrates pre-funded XLM release rules
  • Anchors define how Stellar assets connect to off-chain rails
  • SEP-24 is hosted and interactive; it requires anchor authentication and KYC

MoneyGram Ramps as a target path

  • Not a Bakti partner or current feature
  • Requires allowlisting, SEP-1, SEP-10, SEP-24
  • Requires KYC fields, testing/certification, KYB/compliance, agreements
  • Published off-ramp range: 5–2,500 USDC
  • Malaysia and Philippines listed cash-out only; not proof of Malaysian salary cash-in
Sources: https://developers.stellar.org/docs/learn/fundamentals/anchors · Stellar SEP-24 guide · https://developer.moneygram.com/moneygram-developer/docs/integrate-moneygram-ramps

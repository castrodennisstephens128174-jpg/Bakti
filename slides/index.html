The problem

Remittances reach wallets.
They don't reach habits.

SEA diaspora sends >$100B home each year. Much of it from children supporting elderly parents.

  • Ad-hoc: depends on sender remembering.
  • Apps charge opaque fees; no clean audit.
  • Recipients are often elderly. Can't use a wallet app.
  • A missed month = skipped medicine or groceries.
Source: World Bank Migration & Remittances data (KNOMAD, 2024)
